It might sound gross, but a koi pond is like a toilet bowl — the fish create waste in a closed environment and it must be properly maintained or your fish will get sick and die — it’s that simple. This means you need proper filtration, aeration and circulation to keep your pond running optimally.

The type of equipment and supplies you need will depend on the size of the pond, in addition to your pond’s fish load.

Koi and goldfish pee and poop in the water — this contains ammonia, which is very toxic to your fish health. Without proper filtration, aeration, and circulation, your fish will get sick and die. Koi Market has everything you need to properly maintain your pond.
