about us

Koi Market has the best Koi Fish for Sale Online

The koi represents strength, perseverance, serenity, and a long and healthy life in Japanese culture. At Koi Market Aquatic Gardens, we take these ancient qualities of the koi very seriously and choose and import them with these attributes in mind, offering our clients the highest quality, best koi for sale on the east coast.

From the skill and care we take in hand-choosing and keeping our vast inventory of beautiful and unique koi, to our skilled craftsmanship in customized pond construction and repair, you are sure to find the varieties and services at Koi Market Aquatic Gardens that fit your needs. We don’t stop there. We support your investment with expert maintenance services and a complete array of supplies, so your koi are not only guaranteed to survive but thrive in their new and beautiful environment.

why use koi market

Koi Fish & Gardening Services Serving Nassau and Suffolk

At Koi Market Aquatic Gardens, we are the largest importer of select Japanese koi on the east coast and take great pride in importing from some of the world’s best breeders to ensure our inventory’s beauty, health, and integrity.

We stock over 5,000 koi of many varieties, maintain over 20,000 gallons of bio-secure retail tanks and employ the strictest quarantine and testing procedures to ensure optimal health. Our success has been driven by our unsurpassed procedural compliance, our expert aquaculture support, and the finest in feed and management options to support the long life and health of your koi.

From purchasing your first koi to designing a beautiful koi environment to enhance your property, the experts at Koi Market Aquatic Gardens are here to help you.
